title: The Joy of Plain Text
tags: [writing, tools, markdown]
date: 2024-01-28
---

In an age of complex word processors and bloated formatting options, there's something profoundly satisfying about plain text. It's durable, portable, and timeless.

Markdown, in particular, strikes the perfect balance. It's readable as-is, yet can be transformed into beautiful documents when needed. No lock-in, no proprietary formats, just your words and you.

When I write in plain text, I feel connected to a long tradition of writers, from typewriter to terminal. The focus is on the words, not the toolbar.

Perhaps that's the greatest gift of plain text: it gets out of your way and lets you write.
